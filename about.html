Who Are The Boyos?

Known for their timeless wit and boundless talent, the Boyos are an international group of gamers the likes of which have been seen many, many times before.

The Boyos like to bicker, drink, and mope about playing video games when they should be making something of their lives. Occasionally, when he isn't being a completely useless sack of shit, Jaymes (inarguably the best Boyo), chronicles the group's escapades on BoyosTube.

BoyosTube is, technically speaking, a group channel, created as a clip dump for free use by any of the Boyos, though it was quickly dominated by Jaymes, since he's the least lazy of his friends when it comes to making videos.

If witty banter and video games are two things that interest you, you're probably already subscribed to BoyosTube.
